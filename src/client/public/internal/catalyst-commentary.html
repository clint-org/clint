ECHO-HF interim analysis (n=420 of 1,200)

StoutUpdated 2026-04-29 by Stout

The interim is powered for early stopping on efficacy. We expect the DSMB to continue, not stop. Verzubrix's Phase 2b separation curves were impressive but the underlying mortality signal was driven by the open-label arm; with blinding restored at this scale the effect size should compress to a level that doesn't cross the early-stop boundary.

  • Whether AstraZeneca repeats their Q4 guidance or revises.
