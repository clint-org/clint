Priced per space, with volume pricing, and meant to be flexible. The goal right now is to get Clint in front of your team on a live engagement and shape it around how you work. It starts with a 90-day pilot.

How it works
  • You pay for what you run. A space is one client engagement. One price per space, with no firm license and no minimum. The total goes up only when you add a space.
  • Unlimited users. Every space covers your whole team and the client's people. No per-seat charge.
  • Costs less as you grow. The rate per space drops as you take on more.
