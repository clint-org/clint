Pricing

$25,000 per engagement space, per year

Capped at $300,000 / year firmwide, however many engagements you run.

Priced per engagement, with a cap, and meant to be flexible. The goal right now is to get Clint in front of your team on a live engagement and shape it around how you work. It starts with a 90-day pilot on one engagement.

How it works
  • Per engagement. One space is one client engagement. You pay for the engagements you run on Clint, not a flat platform fee.
  • Unlimited users. Every space includes your whole team and the client's people. No per-seat charge, no counting.
  • Capped. Total cost never exceeds $300,000 a year. Past twelve engagements, additional spaces are included.
