Layout options for the review page

Three proposals for presenting extracted entities before import. All use the same sample data: two existing companies, two existing assets, two existing trials, five new markers, four new events.

Option A: Improved flat list

Same structure as the current page, but with clickable links on existing entities (open in new tab), better field alignment, and a summary bar at the top. Sections remain grouped by type (companies, assets, trials, markers, events).

Source text
Eli Lilly and Company (NYSE: LLY) today announced positive topline results from the ATTAIN-1 Phase 3 clinical trial evaluating orforglipron, an oral GLP-1 receptor agonist, for the treatment of obesity in adults without type 2 diabetes. The trial met its primary endpoint with 14.7% mean body weight reduction versus 2.2% for placebo at 72 weeks.

Separately, Novo Nordisk announced that its Phase 2 trial of amycretin (NNC0487-0111), a novel amylin-GLP-1 dual agonist, achieved 22% mean weight loss at 36 weeks in patients with obesity. The AMBROSIA trial enrolled 450 patients across 40 sites. Novo Nordisk plans to initiate Phase 3 studies of amycretin in Q4 2026.

Eli Lilly plans to submit a New Drug Application to the U.S. FDA in Q3 2026, with a target PDUFA date in Q1 2027.
